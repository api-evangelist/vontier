Prior to her role at Vontier, she served as the chief marketing officer of two of Vontier's lines of business: Invenco by GVR and Gilbarco Veeder-Root. She previously held senior roles at leading organizations like Goldman Sachs and American Express. Elaine holds an MBA in marketing and Finance from the University of Chicago, and a BA in English Literature from Northwestern University. She lives in New York with her family and enjoys recreational running, hitting the slopes, and planning her next family adventure.
